Introducing the Blackbox

As a user of things, is it not inconvenient to have thirty different appliances to do thirty different tasks. Now featuring a new offering from Blackbox Industries, the Blackbox 1.0! The Blackbox does everything you've ever wanted, but in one single package. By using space age materials, sintered multi-phase inductors, the Internet of things, and quantum computing, we've even managed to compress the physical footprint of the Blackbox into the size of a small box. Of course no one package fits every user, so if our standard offerings don't meet your needs, we also offer custom built Blackboxes.

Here's some of the features you'll get when you buy a Blackbox:

  • Quick Charge
  • The official Blackbox app for phone connectivity
  • Noise canceling
  • 4800 DPI scanning
  • CD drive
  • High traction tires
